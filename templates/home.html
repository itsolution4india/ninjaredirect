Crispy Golden Samosa Recipe
October 31, 2025

About the Recipe

Samosa — a golden, crispy, deep-fried snack filled with a spicy potato mixture — is one of the most loved street foods in India. Whether served with mint chutney or tangy tamarind sauce, this recipe guarantees that perfect crunch and authentic flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 medium boiled potatoes, mashed
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 2 tbsp oil (for dough)
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Dough: Mix flour, salt, and oil. Add water gradually to form a firm dough. Let it rest for 20 minutes.
  2. Make the Filling: Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, then sauté ginger-garlic paste. Add mashed potatoes, peas, and spices. Mix well and cool.
  3. Shape the Samosas: Roll small circles of dough, cut in half, form a cone, fill with potato mixture, and seal the edges.
  4. Fry: Deep fry in medium-hot oil until golden brown and crispy.
  5. Serve: Enjoy hot with mint chutney or tamarind sauce!

These homemade samosas are crunchy outside, soft and spicy inside — perfect for tea-time or evening snacks.
